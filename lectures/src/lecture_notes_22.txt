## Leading lighting technologies
- Incandescent
- Fluorescent
- Light Emitting Diodes


## Incandescent bulbs
- Tungsten filament
- Heated to high temperature
- Inert gas to prevent oxidation
- Close to blackbody spectrum
- Much of spectrum in the IR

## Fluorescent bulbs
- Current flows through vapor
- Uses phosphor to convert UV photons to visible
- Spectrum does not resemble black body
